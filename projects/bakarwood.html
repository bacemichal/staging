Bakarwood (design & dev)

Bakarwood is a small family business focused on handcrafted epoxy furniture.

Their approach is rooted in tradition – respect for materials, honest work, and attention to detail. This philosophy shaped the structure and tone of the website.

One of the key ideas behind the design is that furniture can be a reflection of individuality. An interactive section with epoxy samples invites visitors to explore and imagine how their piece could look.

The site is built with future growth in mind – it's ready for a German language version, an online shop, and a CMS for product management and blog content.

Service: ui/ux, webflow development, photography, content strategy.
