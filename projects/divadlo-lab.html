Divadlo lab (design)

“Divadlo Lab is a modern and innovative student theatre that provides a space for presenting the work of students from the Theatre Faculty of VŠMU to the general public.”— source: Divadlo Lab

Divadlo Lab is a space for theatrical experimentation and exploration – its name (“Lab”) directly reflects that character.

The goal of the design was to express that character visually. The result is a non-conventional yet functional and flexible website, built to handle a large volume of content and diverse forms of presentation.

The project was created in collaboration with Stella Digit
