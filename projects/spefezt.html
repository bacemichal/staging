Spefezt (brand)

The event branding was based on the established visual language of the Feztmind identity.

The event logo is a custom wordmark, with colors changing each year to visually distinguish each edition.

Social media posts were created to communicate core event info — guests, competition format, crew, and schedule. Story templates were also designed and used during the event for real-time updates.

This year marks the third edition of the event.

Together with Feztmind, we’re working on new concepts to improve the experience, bring something fresh, and support the Slovak parkour community.
